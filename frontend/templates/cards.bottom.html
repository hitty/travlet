From Gatwick Airport

  1. Take Gatwick Express to Victoria Station.
  2. Change to Underground – Circle Line to Notting Hill Gate OR Victoria Line to Oxford Circus.
  3. Change to Central Line to North Acton (steps to exit)
  4. Walk or take bus 266 (towards Hammersmith) to hotel – 0.8 miles; 15-20 minutes walk (see map above)

Driving

We are located off the A4020 (Acton High Street) and A40 (Western Avenue) which runs through London and is not part of the congestion charging zone.

Parking

We have our own car park at the rear of the building. This is free of charge for guests during their stay. If there are no spaces, the street parking is free from 5pm – 10am next day, Monday to Friday. Parking is free all day on Saturday and Sunday.

From Heathrow Airport (For all terminals)

Take TfL Rail (from Heathrow Terminals 2&3 or 4 Rail Station) to Acton Main Line Rail Station.
